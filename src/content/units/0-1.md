A million seconds is about 11 days. A billion seconds is about 32 years. The gap between a million and a billion is not the gap between one and a thousand: it is the gap between a fortnight and a human lifetime. Most public figures are presented at this scale, and most people have no felt sense of what the numbers mean.

This unit builds that felt sense. By the end, you will be able to place any number in context — and spot when context has been deliberately withheld.
