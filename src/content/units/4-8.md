Linda seems like a feminist bank teller, so she is judged more likely to be one than a plain bank teller. The cab seemed Green because the witness's testimony sounded credible, and the Green prior was ignored. Both errors stem from the same source: the mind uses resemblance to a prototype as a proxy for probability. It is a fast, often useful heuristic that fails in systematic, predictable ways.
