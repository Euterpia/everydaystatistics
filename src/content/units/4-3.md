Linda is 31, single, outspoken, and deeply concerned with issues of discrimination and social justice. Which is more probable: (A) Linda is a bank teller, or (B) Linda is a bank teller who is active in the feminist movement?

More than 80% of people choose (B). This is formally impossible. P(A and B) cannot exceed P(A). The vividness of the description overrides the mathematical constraint, every time.
