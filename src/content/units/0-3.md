If 99 workers earn £30,000 and one CEO earns £3,000,000, the mean wage is approximately £60,000. Nobody in the room earns that. The median wage — the middle value — is £30,000, which accurately describes what a typical employee takes home.

Both figures are mathematically correct. Only one is honest for the stated purpose. This unit introduces mean, median, and mode, and shows why the choice between them is never neutral.
