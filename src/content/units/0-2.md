A change from 2% to 4% is a 100% increase and a 2 percentage point increase. These are not the same statement. One sounds dramatic; the other is modest. Both are technically accurate. Knowing which one is being used — and why — is the first skill in reading statistics honestly.

This unit covers percentages, fractions, ratios, and the denominator problem: a percentage is meaningless without knowing what it is a percentage of.
