Per capita chocolate consumption correlates strongly with Nobel Prize laureates per country. Ice cream sales correlate with drowning rates. Countries with more televisions per household have lower birth rates. Every correlation has at least three possible explanations. Choosing one requires evidence beyond the correlation itself.

The word "associated with" in a scientific headline always means correlated. It never means caused. Every time you see it, ask which of the three explanations is most likely.
