A graph is not a neutral representation of data. The choice of y-axis starting point, x-axis range, chart type, and scale all shape the visual impression before any number is read. These choices are rarely accidental.

This unit covers the truncated axis, the compressed time scale, the dual axis, the area distortion, and the missing baseline. By the end, you will see these tricks immediately.
