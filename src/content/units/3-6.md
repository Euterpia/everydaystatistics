The majority of psychology studies are conducted on WEIRD participants: Western, Educated, Industrialised, Rich, Democratic. These studies are then published as findings about human cognition. Drug trials have historically been conducted predominantly on male participants; the results have been applied to women. These are not minor caveats. They are structural failures of generalisation.
